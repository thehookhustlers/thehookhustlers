Our Mission

Democratising Access to Capital for Every Indian Startup

India has over 6 lakh registered startups. Less than 2% ever get funded. The system was broken — opaque, metro-biased and inaccessible to founders from Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities who have incredible ideas but no connections.

The Hook Hustlers exists to change that. We built a platform where any DPIIT-verified startup — from Lucknow to Vishakhapatnam, from Indore to Imphal — can connect directly with the right investor, at the right time, for the right amount.

No gatekeepers. No geography bias. Just great startups meeting great capital.

Founder & CEO · The Hook Hustlers
"Why should a brilliant founder from Varanasi have fewer funding opportunities than one from Bandra? I built this because the answer should be: they shouldn't."

At 17, watching incredible startups from smaller cities die quietly — not because the ideas were bad, but because they couldn't find the right investors — The Hook Hustlers was born from that frustration.

This isn't a weekend project. This is a mission — to build the infrastructure India's startup ecosystem deserves. Every feature, every partnership, every line of code is in service of one goal: connecting the right idea to the right capital.
