2. Platform Description

The Hook Hustlers is an online startup-investor matchmaking platform operating in India. We connect DPIIT-verified startups with verified investors including angel investors, venture capital firms, corporate investors, government funds, and private equity firms. The Platform provides tools for matching, communication, deal tracking, and documentation.

3. Eligibility & Verification

To list as a startup on the Platform, you must:

  • Be registered as a startup under the DPIIT Startup India scheme with a valid DPIIT registration number
  • Upload valid DPIIT registration documents during the signup process
  • Provide accurate and truthful information about your company
  • Be authorised to represent the company you are registering

To list as an investor, you must provide valid LinkedIn profile and AUM (Assets Under Management) proof for verification by our team.

Commission rates are as follows:

  • ₹1 Lakh – ₹3 Lakh: 3-4% of deal value
  • ₹3 Lakh – ₹5 Lakh: 2-3% of deal value
  • ₹5 Lakh – ₹10 Lakh: 1.5% of deal value
  • ₹10 Lakh – ₹20 Lakh: 1% of deal value
  • ₹20 Lakh – ₹50 Lakh: 0.75-1% of deal value
  • ₹50 Lakh – ₹1 Crore: 0.5% of deal value
  • ₹1 Crore and above: Flat fee of ₹75,000

Commission is payable by the startup within 30 days of deal confirmation on the Platform.
